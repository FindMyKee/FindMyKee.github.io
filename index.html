Locate And Ring Your Keychain.

After pairing your phone to the keychain, it will automatically reconnect when in range and store it's last location to display on Maps within the app. When you are connected you can ring the device for a 90 decibel alarm to trigger. Using the distance label you can tell when you are getting closer or farther away.

Single-Action Button Rings Phone.

Reverse the direction! If you have your keys, but lost your phone, just click the keychain button and your phone will start ringing within range.

Latest Bluetooth Technology.

Low-energy Bluetooth 5 is backwards compatible with Bluetooth 1, 2, 3, and 4 and saves your keychain battery for more than 12 months.

Ultra Thin

The dimensions of the keychain are 45mm x 45mm x 3.2mm. Fits on most keychain rings and ships with tie connector for handbags, suitcases, and more.
